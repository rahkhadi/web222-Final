    SHORT AUTOBIOGRAPHY

    Hello there, I am Rahimullah Khadim Hussain. I have always been a huge fan of games, both indoor and outdoor, since my childhood. One of my favorite games to play with friends and family is Rock Paper Scissors. It is a simple yet fun game that requires no special equipment and can be played anywhere, anytime. That's why I decided to create this website, to share my love for this game and help others enjoy it too.

    Motive behind the website?

    The reason for creating this website is to spread awareness about this simple yet exciting game that can bring people together and have fun. Rock Paper Scissors is a game that transcends age, gender, and culture, making it an excellent way to bond with friends, family, and even strangers. Through this website, I aim to provide information about the game, its rules, and how to play it. Additionally, I will be sharing tips and strategies to help you win more games.
