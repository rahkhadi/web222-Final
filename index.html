Levels of rock paper scissors

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple game that is enjoyed by people of all ages. The game is based on the idea of two players making a hand gesture at the same time and determining the winner based on the gestures. While the basic game involves just three hand gestures - rock, paper, and scissors - there are different variations of the game that involve more hand gestures and complex rules.
